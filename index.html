ABOUT ME!

Hi! I’m Katie McLean, a second year student at the University of Waterloo studying Global Business and Digital Arts. I’m an avid learner and creative soul, always looking for new opportunities.

I love photography, and I usually shoot on either a Nikon D7000, a Canon R7, or my good old iPhone 12. I like to think I have a good eye for composition, and I try to showcase this skill in my photography often.

I'm always trying to build on my photography skillset, playing around with different types of photography including product shoots, landscapes, nature shots, action photography, portraits, and more.

~ Katie McLean
